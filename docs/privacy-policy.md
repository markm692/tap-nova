# Privacy Policy for Creator Empire

**Last updated: March 22, 2026**

## Overview

Creator Empire is an offline idle clicker game. We respect your privacy.

## Data Collection

Creator Empire does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- **No personal information** is collected
- **No analytics or tracking** software is included
- **No ads** are displayed
- **No internet connection** is required to play
- **No data is sent** to any server

## Local Storage

Game progress is saved locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

## Third-Party Services

Creator Empire does not use any third-party services that collect data.

## Children's Privacy

Creator Empire does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.
