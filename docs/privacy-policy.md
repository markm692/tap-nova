# Privacy Policy for Tap Nova

**Last updated: March 21, 2026**

## Overview

Tap Nova is an offline idle clicker game. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency.

## Data Collection

Tap Nova does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected (name, email, phone, etc.)
- **No analytics or tracking** software is included
- **No ads** are displayed
- **No internet connection** is required to play
- **No data is sent** to any server

## Local Storage

Game progress (score, upgrades, achievements) is saved locally on your device using the app's internal storage. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app
- Cannot be accessed by other apps

## Third-Party Services

Tap Nova does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect data.

## Children's Privacy

Tap Nova does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here.
